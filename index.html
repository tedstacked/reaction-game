How to Play

Instructions

Test your reaction time! Click the 3D shape when it turns green. Wait for the color change - clicking too early will result in a false start.

Reaction Time Ratings

  • Incredible < 200ms
  • Excellent 200 - 250ms
  • Great 250 - 300ms
  • Good 300 - 400ms
  • Average 400 - 500ms
  • Below Average > 500ms

Tips

The average human reaction time is around 250ms. Professional gamers often achieve times below 200ms. Focus on the shape and stay relaxed!
